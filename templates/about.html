About

There have been many wrestlers over the years but who are the people behind the masks, and where are they from? Who are the best wrestlers of all time? The purpose of this website is to provide visualizations of data related to wrestlers. We used two data sets (see links below), one from kaggle.com and one from cagematch.net.
